Oxfordshire Art Society
Members' Exhibition
26th March - 10th April 2022
Kendrew Barn Gallery, St John's College, St Giles, Oxford OX1 3JP

This is a selected exhibition in the beautiful surroundings of Kendrew Barn Gallery. I'm delighted to have two layered papercuts included amongst some very high quality work.

This link will take you to the St John's College website where, if you scroll down, you will find on the right a link to a plan of the college.
The entrance to the barn gallery is on the right hand side if you are heading away from Oxford on St Giles, a little further down than the war memorial where St Giles divides into Woodstock Road and Banbury Road.
