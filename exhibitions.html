See my work in 2022:

Royal Academy Summer Exhibition
21st June - 21st Aug

I'm delighted to have one of my altered maps in this year's Summer Exhibition. You can see 'We Came to be Here' and the rest of this exciting exhibition ubtil 21st Aug 2022.


The Cutting Edge: Matisse and Contemporary Paper Artists
28th May - 4th Sept 2022

Rheged Centre,
Cumbria
CA11 0DQ

I'm thrilled to have 4 pieces of work in this exhibition that highlights the work of 40 contemporary  British paper artists alongside "Matisse: Drawing with Scissors - late works 1950-1954", a Hayward Gallery Touring Exhibition, which features 35 lithographic prints of the famous cut-outs produced by Henri Matisse.
Two of my altered map sculptures will be shown alongside two of my layered papercut moons.


'Celebrating 40 Years' Artweeks at the Jam Factory
26th April - 19th June 2022

Celebrating Artweeks' 40th anniversary with a large exhibition of joyful and colourful art by 100 artists all of whom have their own exhibitions around the county during May. I'm delighted to have had one of my layered papercuts accepted for this fabulous exhibition.
