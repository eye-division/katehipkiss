See my work in 2022:


The Cutting Edge: Matisse and Contemporary Paper Artists
28th May - 4th Sept 2022

Rheged Centre,
Cumbria
CA11 0DQ

I'm thrilled to have 4 pieces of work in this exhibition that highlights the work of 40 contemporary  British paper artists alongside "Matisse: Drawing with Scissors - late works 1950-1954", a Hayward Gallery Touring Exhibition, which features 35 lithographic prints of the famous cut-outs produced by Henri Matisse.
Two of my altered map sculptures will be shown alongside two of my layered papercut moons.
 

Kingfisher Art
14 New Street, Chipping Norton OX7 5LJ

You can find a collection of my layered papercuts at this beautiful gallery in Chipping Norton.


Atelier by the Sea Open Exhibition
14th July - 3rd Sept

165 Kings Road Arches
Brighton Beach
BN1 1NB

This Open Exhibition features the work of 130 artists and I am pleased to have 2 layered papercuts being shown.


Open Exhibition, Oxford Art Society
20th Aug - 4th Sept

SJE, Iffley Road, Oxford, OX4 1EH

Two of my layered papercuts - 'Grazing on Portmeadow' and 'Stag. Broadway Tower' - will be included in this exhibition annual exhibition.
