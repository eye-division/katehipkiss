Current and Upcoming:
 

Viviane Fontaine International Paper Triennial

Musée Charmey, Gruyère, Switzerland

11th March - 12th November 2023

I am honoured to have been awarded 3rd prize in this Triennial exhibition for my altered atlas sculpture 'Breathe'.
 

Oxford Art Society Members' Exhibition

Kendrew Barn, St John's College, St Giles, Oxford OX1 3JP

18th March - 2nd April
11am - 6pm daily

I have 2 pieces of work in this annual exhibition; one layered landscape and a cut-map globe. The on-line catalogue will be available soon.


Delamore Arts

Delamore Estate, Cornwood, Devon

1st - 31st May 2023

I will be showing a small collection of layered landscapes and an altered atlas in this annual exhibition in beautiful surroundings!


Oxfordshire Artweeks

Jericho, Oxford

20th - 29th May 2023

This year I'm excited to be exhibiting my work in Jericho with glass artist Wendy Newhofer and jeweller Becky Morgans.
 

Kingfisher Art
14 New Street, Chipping Norton OX7 5LJ

You can find a collection of my layered papercuts at this beautiful gallery in Chipping Norton
