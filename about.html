I work with paper, cutting intricate patterns and shapes to exploit the qualities of the material and create delicate, complex layers full of movement and depth.

Inspired by architecture, landscape and the natural world, I am especially interested in forms within the landscape created both by natural forces, such as flowing water, and human exploits like mining and farming; the shaping of our landscape over time.
